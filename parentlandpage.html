Parent Note
YouthSaver allows your child to have a bank account and a Debit Card to make savings and spending decisions monitored and controlled by the parent.Children will set a goal by selecting the item(s) they wish to redeem. The debit card will allow the child to deposit their savings from an allowance given by parents or parents providing monetary incentive to the child for doing something good (E.g., Good Grades, Doing household chores).Debit card activates only when the goal is reached and “checked out” by the child. Left over from purchases will be placed into their deposit account which acts as force savings. Withdrawals are allowed only upon parent's approval.
